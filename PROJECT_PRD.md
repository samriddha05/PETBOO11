# 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Project:** PetSphere – AI-Powered Pet Health Operating System  
**Document Scope:** MVP-1 Detailed Project Plan  
**MVP-1 Focus Modules:** Tele-Vet Consultations, Fresh Food Delivery System, AI Assistant (Cuddles), PetSphere Community (Pet Social)  
**Target Launch Market:** Hyderabad  
**Target Users:** Urban pet parents, first-time owners, health-conscious users  

This document provides the comprehensive project plan for MVP-1 of PetSphere, focusing on four core modules: Tele-Vet consultations for instant veterinary access, a Fresh Food Delivery system for pet meals with an on-demand ordering experience, and Cuddles – an AI-powered pet care assistant.

---

## 1.1 MVP-1 Objectives

| Objective | Description |
|----------|------------|
| Tele-Vet Consultations | On-demand and scheduled video/chat consultations with verified veterinarians |
| Fresh Food Delivery | Pet fresh food ordering & delivery system |
| Cuddles AI Assistant | Conversational AI chatbot for pet care guidance |
| PetSphere Community | Pet social platform for sharing moments |

# 2. MVP-1 SCOPE DEFINITION

MVP-1 includes four major modules along with supporting infrastructure.

---

## 2.1 Module A: Tele-Vet Consultations

### A1. Vet Directory & Discovery
- Vet listing with profiles (specialization, experience, ratings)
- Search and filter options
- Availability calendar
- Detailed vet profile page

### A2. Appointment Booking
- Scheduled consultations
- On-demand consultations
- Notifications for confirmation
- Reschedule & cancel options
- Pre-consultation form

### A3. Video & Chat Consultation
- HD video calls
- Chat consultation option
- File/image sharing
- Auto-reconnect system

### A4. Post-Consultation
- Summary & recommendations
- Digital prescriptions
- Consultation history
- Follow-up reminders
- Rating system

---

## 2.2 Module B: Fresh Food Delivery System

### B1. Delivery Model
- Morning orders → same-day delivery
- Evening orders → next-day delivery
- Slot-based delivery

### B2. Food Menu
- Categorized menu (meals, snacks, diets)
- Nutritional info & ingredients
- Pet-specific filtering

### B3. Ordering Flow
- Browse → cart → slot → payment
- Address management
- Coupon support
- Multiple payment methods

### B4. Order Tracking
- Real-time status updates
- Live tracking
- Delivery confirmation

### B5. Post-Order
- Order history
- Reviews & ratings
- Refund handling
- Support chat

### B6. Seller/Kitchen Panel (Website Dashboard)
- Partner onboarding
- Menu management
- Order tracking
- Revenue dashboard

---

## 2.3 Module C: AI Assistant – Cuddles

### Features
- Conversational chatbot
- Personalized responses using pet data
- Health & behavior guidance
- Escalation to tele-vet
- Chat history retention

---

## 2.4 Module D: PetSphere Community

### D1. Pet Profiles
- Pet identity profiles
- Multiple pets per user
- Activity badges

### D2. Social Feed
- Posts, likes, comments
- Hashtags
- Content moderation

### D3. Interaction
- Follow pets
- Playdate requests
- Messaging
- Community challenges

---

## 2.5 Supporting Infrastructure

### User Management
- Registration with OTP
- Social login
- Pet profiles
- JWT authentication

### Payments
- Razorpay integration
- Order & consultation payments
- Refund system

### Notifications
- Push notifications (FCM)
- SMS & email alerts

### Dashboard (Website)
- User profile
- Order history
- Pet management
- Settings
